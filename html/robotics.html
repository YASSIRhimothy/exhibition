Welcome to the Robotics Page

What are robots

Robots are machines that are designed to do tasks automatically. Some robots can follow instructions exactly, while others can think and make decisions using artificial intelligence. They can look like humans, animals, or just machines built for a specific job.

Will robots take over people`s jobs

Robots and machines are changing how people work really fast, but that doesn’t mean they’ll take all the jobs. Instead, robots will help by doing dangerous and repetitive jobs that people don’t want to do like lifting heavy stuff in factories or organizing big piles of packages. This gives people more time to do the fun, creative, or caring jobs that robots aren’t good at!

Robotics is the branch of technology that deals with the design, construction, and operation of robots.
